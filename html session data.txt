4.Html First Program:
===================
1..Change body background color:
===========================
Approach1:
==========
syntax:
	<body bgcolor="ColorName">
Ex:
	<body bgcolor="orange">
Approach2: By color code(Hexa decimal format)
==========
		R	G	B

		00	00	00	=>	Black
		00	00	FF	=>	Blue
		FF	00	00	=>	Red
		00	FF	00	=>	green
		00	FF	FF	=>	Green & Blue
		FF	FF	FF	=>	White
syntax:
	<body bgcolor="#ColorCode">
Ex:
	<body bgcolor="#00FF00">
